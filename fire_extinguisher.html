Choosing the Right Fire Extinguisher

Fire extinguishers are classified based on the type of fire they can extinguish:

  • Class A: Ordinary combustibles like wood, paper, cloth
  • Class B: Flammable liquids like gasoline, oil, grease
  • Class C: Electrical equipment
  • Class D: Combustible metals
  • Class K: Cooking oils and fats (kitchen fires)
