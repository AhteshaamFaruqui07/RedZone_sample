Our Story

From a small team to industry leaders

2010

Founded

Red Zone Security Solutions Born

Founded in Jabalpur, Madhya Pradesh, Red Zone started with a simple mission: to provide reliable fire safety equipment to local businesses. Our dedication to quality and customer service quickly earned us a reputation as a trusted partner in the security industry.

2015

Expansion

Regional Growth

As demand for our products grew, we expanded our operations across central India. We began offering a wider range of security solutions, including advanced surveillance systems, access control, and integrated security management.

2020

Innovation

Embracing Technology

We incorporated cutting-edge technology into our offerings, including AI-powered cameras, smart fire suppression systems, and IoT-enabled security devices. This technological advancement positioned us as innovators in the field.

2026

Today

Industry Leaders

Today, Red Zone stands as a premier provider of fire safety and security solutions in India. Our commitment to excellence, continuous innovation, and customer satisfaction drives everything we do. We're not just selling products; we're delivering peace of mind.
